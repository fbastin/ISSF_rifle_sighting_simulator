ISSF Rifle Sighting Simulator

A browser-based pedagogical tool that models the optical and ballistic phenomena encountered in ISSF precision rifle marksmanship. Isolate and observe six fundamental effects: parallax error, aperture and eye-relief optics, exterior ballistics (gravity drop), the canting paradox, mechanical sight adjustment (clicks), and wind drift.
